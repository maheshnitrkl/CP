📚 Stack & Queue Fundamentals

Stack (LIFO): Last In, First Out. Operations: push, pop, top — all O(1).

Queue (FIFO): First In, First Out. Operations: enqueue, dequeue, front — all O(1).

💡 Monotonic Stack Pattern
When you need the next greater/smaller element, use a monotonic stack. Iterate through the array; while the stack top is smaller (or larger) than the current element, pop and record the answer.
